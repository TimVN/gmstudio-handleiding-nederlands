Time Lines

This section deals with time line functions.

Time lines are a powerful mechanism for controlling events in your game, and this section covers the different ways that you can deal with them in your game.

Timeline Functions

The following functions are for adding and manipulating Timelines from within your game:

  1. timeline_exists
  2. timeline_get_name
  3. timeline_add
  4. timeline_delete
  5. timeline_moment_clear

Timeline Instance Variables

The following variables are "built in" to all instances to make working with Timelines easier:

  1. timeline_index
  2. timeline_running
  3. timeline_speed
  4. timeline_position
  5. timeline_loop
